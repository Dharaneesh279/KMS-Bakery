SIGNATURE
 INGREDIANTS

Crafted with passion and purity – our signature sweets are made using time-honored recipes and the finest natural ingredients.
Each creation is slow-cooked to perfection, offering a melt-in-mouth experience that captures the essence of tradition, taste, and wholesome indulgence.

Karupati Halva

Rich, authentic, and wholesome – made with the natural
sweetness of palm jaggery, slow-cooked to perfection for a
melt-in-mouth delight. A traditional treat that's both
healthy and heavenly.

Banana Pudding

Creamy, comforting, and irresistibly smooth – layered with the
natural sweetness of ripe bananas and velvety custard. A nostalgic dessert
that brings warmth and joy in every spoonful,
simple yet utterly satisfying.

Milk Sweets

Pure, indulgent, and time-honored – crafted from the richness
of fresh milk, slow-stirred to perfection for a soft, melt-in-mouth
experience. A classic delicacy that celebrates
tradition in every creamy bite.
